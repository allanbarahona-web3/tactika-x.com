Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical knowledge to use the platform?
No! Our platform is designed for everyone. Choose a template, add your products, and customize colors and text. It's as easy as using social media.
Can I use my own domain name?
Yes! On the Professional and Enterprise plans, you can connect your own domain (mystore.com). We provide step-by-step instructions to set it up.
What payment methods do you support?
We include integrated payment processing for credit/debit cards and PayPal. On Professional and Enterprise plans, you can also connect your own Stripe or PayPal account for lower fees.
What happens if I don't pay my subscription?
Your store will be suspended after 5 days of non-payment. Your data is kept safe for 30 days, giving you time to update your payment method. After 30 days, the store and data will be permanently deleted.
Can I change templates after launching?
Yes! You can change templates anytime. Your products and data are preserved. Just pick a new template, adjust colors, and publish. Takes only minutes.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. Test all features, add products, and see if it's right for you. Cancel anytime.
